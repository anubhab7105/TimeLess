// OUR MISSION

TIME IS THE ONLY CURRENCY THAT MATTERS

We started TimeLess to challenge the idea that premium timepieces are reserved for the elite. Great design should be accessible. Precision engineering should be wearable. And style — that's for everyone.

Each watch in our collection goes through a rigorous quality check before it reaches your wrist. We work with independent watchmakers, emerging designers, and street artists to create pieces that live at the intersection of function and culture.

  • 100% authentic, curated timepieces from indie and premium brands
  • Direct-to-consumer pricing — no middlemen, no markups
  • 2-year warranty on every watch we sell
  • Free returns within 30 days, no questions asked

// OUR JOURNEY

HOW WE GOT HERE

2019

THE GARAGE DAYS

Founders Dev & Riya start curating watches from indie brands out of a garage in Lajpat Nagar. First month: 12 orders. All to friends.

FIRST COLLAB DROP

Partnered with Delhi street artist KRNZ for a limited 50-piece collaboration. Sold out in 4 hours. The internet noticed.

2020
2021

₹1 CRORE MILESTONE

Hit ₹1 crore in annual revenue. Moved from a garage to a proper studio. Hired our first full-time team of 5. Still chaotic, still pure.

NATIONAL EXPANSION

Launched shipping to all 28 states and 8 UTs. Opened our first pop-up store in Connaught Place. 3,000 visitors in one weekend.

2022
2023

GLOBAL LAUNCH

Began shipping internationally to 40+ countries. Launched the TimeLess app with 50K downloads in the first week.

TODAY — AND BEYOND

50,000+ customers. 100+ unique designs. Still the same spirit. Still the same garage hustle energy. This is only chapter one.
